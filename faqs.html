Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We are accepting online payment like gcash, gotyme, maya and other banks like union bank, landbank, BDO, and Mastercard.

How long will it take for my order to arrive?

It depende on your location but if your within metro manila it will take 2-4 days except for non-working holidays and 5-7 days for provincial.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes, we just need your receipt.

Do you offer discounts for bulk orders?

No, but we do have sales and half percent off.

How do I know which size fits me?

There is a size guide of chart on the shopping cart before you placed your order you can check the waistline and the length of the shirt.
